Meet Richa Jindal

Hi! I'm Richa Jindal, a student at Boston University. This Chocolate Personality Quiz is my project for CM523, where I'm exploring how interactive design can create engaging and thoughtful user experiences.

The idea came from my love of chocolate and personality quizzes. I wanted to create something fun that combines both - helping people discover their "chocolate personality" while learning about different chocolate types and pairings.

Through this project, I'm experimenting with user flow, interactive elements, and responsive design to create an experience that's both educational and entertaining.
